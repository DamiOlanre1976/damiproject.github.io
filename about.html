About Me

Hello! I’m Damilola Lasode, a passionate and results-driven Data Analyst with a strong background in data-driven problem-solving, business intelligence, and reporting. I specialize in transforming complex datasets into clear, actionable insights that drive strategic decisions and improve business performance.

I hold a master’s degree in International Logistics and Supply Chain Management and have developed extensive experience working with data across various industries. My career journey has been shaped by my analytical skills, ability to interpret data trends, and commitment to continuous learning.In my previous roles, I have successfully analyzed large datasets to uncover hidden opportunities, streamline processes, and enhance decision-making. Whether it’s analyzing user behavior, tracking key performance indicators (KPIs), or visualizing data for stakeholders, I thrive on turning raw data into meaningful stories. I have hands-on experience with tools like Excel, Power BI, Tableau and SQL, enabling me to extract, clean, and analyze data effectively.

Skills and Expertise:

Data Visualization & Business Intelligence:

  • Proficient in building interactive dashboards and reports using, Excel, Power BI and Tableau.
  • Expert at presenting complex findings in clear, user-friendly formats for executives and non-technical teams.

Data Analysis & Modeling:

  • Skilled at analyzing large, structured, and unstructured datasets to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights.
  • Experience in predictive modeling, regression analysis, and A/B testing.

SQL & Database Management:

  • Strong ability to query databases, manipulate large datasets, and optimize data performance.

Business Process Optimization:

  • Adept at identifying process bottlenecks and opportunities for efficiency through data-driven solutions.

Professional Experience:

During my time at Sosadales Limited, I collaborated with cross-functional teams to analyze business data, monitor KPIs, and design insightful dashboards that improved operational efficiency by 25%. I also led data analysis initiatives that contributed to an increase in customer retention or revenue growth (e.g., through better user behavior analysis).

In another role at 10Alytics, I analyzed user feedback data, merged analytics across multiple data sources, and identified trends that directly influenced key product improvements and customer satisfaction.

What Drives Me:

I have a deep passion for solving problems using data. I believe that every dataset tells a story, and my role is to uncover that story to help businesses make informed decisions. I take pride in my ability to bridge the gap between raw data and strategic business outcomes.

Additionally, I enjoy learning new technologies, tools, and methods that keep me agile in the ever-changing world of data analytics. Whether it’s working with large-scale datasets, automating repetitive processes, or enhancing business operations, I bring curiosity and dedication to every project.

My Vision:

I aim to leverage my analytical skills to drive data-informed decisions that create real-world impact. My goal is to contribute to organizations that value innovation, data literacy, and strategic growth. As I continue to grow in my data analytics career, I strive to mentor others, develop best practices, and stay at the forefront of data technologies.

Outside of Work:

When I’m not analyzing data, you can find me exploring my passion for sustainable practices and supply chain innovations, reflecting my academic background. I also enjoy volunteering, learning about new technologies, and staying active in professional networks that foster data analytics growth.

Let’s connect! I’m excited about opportunities where I can apply my skills to help businesses solve challenges, unlock value, and achieve measurable success through data-driven strategies.
